About Us

The COEP Gymkhana is a central hub for student activities at the College of Engineering Pune (COEP), facilitating a wide range of sports, cultural, and technical pursuits. It serves as a platform for students to engage in extracurricular activities, promoting holistic development alongside academic excellence.

  • Sports Infrastructure: The Gymkhana encompasses a 6.5-acre ground utilized for sports such as cricket, hockey, volleyball, football, tennis, and jogging. Additionally, there are floodlit basketball and tennis courts that enable extended play into the evening hours.
  • Hostel Proximity: The Gymkhana ground is conveniently located near the hostel blocks, providing students with easy access to sports and recreational facilities.
  • Student Clubs: Under the Gymkhana's aegis, various student clubs operate, including the Software Development Section (SDS), which focuses on software development projects and technical skill enhancement.

Events and Initiatives:

  • Zest Festival: The Gymkhana organizes 'Zest,' COEP's annual sports festival, attracting participation from numerous colleges and featuring a variety of sports competitions.
  • Gymkhana Day: An annual event celebrating student achievements in sports and cultural activities, fostering community spirit and showcasing talent.
  • Administration: The Gymkhana is managed by university functionaries, including a Vice-President responsible for overseeing its operations.

In summary, the COEP Gymkhana plays a pivotal role in enriching the student experience by providing diverse opportunities for engagement beyond the classroom, thereby contributing to the overall development of its students.

How far in advance can I book a venue?

You can book a venue up to 2 months in advance.

Is there a limit to the number of bookings one can make?

No, there is no limit on the bookings one can make

Can I modify or cancel a booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel a booking as per the guidelines set by the administration.

If multiple people try to book the same venue for the same timings, who gets the booking?

The college authorities will review requests and grant permissions accordingly.

Why do I need to select two alternate venues while booking?

This ensures that if your first choice is unavailable, your request is automatically considered for the second or third venue, reducing delays.
