How far in advance can I book a venue?

You can book a venue up to 2 months in advance.

Is there a limit to the number of bookings one can make?

Yes, you can make a maximum of 3 bookings per week.

Can I modify or cancel a booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel a booking as per the guidelines set by the administration.

If multiple people try to book the same venue for the same timings, who gets the booking?

The college authorities will review requests and grant permissions accordingly.

Why do I need to select two alternate venues while booking?

This ensures that if your first choice is unavailable, your request is automatically considered for the second or third venue, reducing delays.
